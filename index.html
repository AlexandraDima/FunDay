About FUN YOGA

We are looking forward to welcoming you to Fun Yoga event the first weekend of September 2018.

Fun Yoga is an event that involves physical activity, where people meet and have fun. Bring your friends and join us at Kolding Bypark, on 2nd September 2018.

Fun Yoga Yoga event is about making yoga more accessible to all, as well as gathering and displaying yoga diversity in one place.

IBA is a International Business Academy providing interesting study programs in Kolding
This event is about having fun and to be part of social life in Kolding
